1. Place the eggs in a pot in a single layer and cover them with cold water by 1 inch.
2. Boil the water by Medium-High heat.
3. After the water starts boiling, continue boiling for 5 minutes.
4. Turn off the heat. Keep the pot on the heat burner and let it sit for 1 to 2 minutes till the water bubbles disappear.
5. Drain the hot water and take out the eggs. Run cold water over the eggs to cool them. Peel and eat!
